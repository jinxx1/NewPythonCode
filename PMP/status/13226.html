158、A demanding stakeholder insists on adding project requirements. The project manager wants to accommodate the requests, but many of the requirements exceed the budget and are misaligned with business needs. What should the project manager do?

一名严苛的项目相关方坚持要增加项目需求。项目经理希望满足该请求，但是许多需求超出预算并与商业需求不一致。项目经理应该怎么做？

A、 Approve only the requests that are within budget and align with business needs 仅批准符合预算并与商业需求一致的请求

B、 Forward the requests to the change control board (CCB) 将请求提交给变更控制委员会（CCB）

C、 Meet with the sponsor to review the requests against the project scope statement 与项目发起人开会，根据项目范围说明书评审请求

D、 Deny the requests and inform the stakeholder 拒绝请求，并通知项目相关方
