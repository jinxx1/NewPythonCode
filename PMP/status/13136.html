68、A new project manager joins an ongoing project. During the knowledge transfer sessions.The project manager realizes that the business requirements are unclear and differ from the former project manager’s understanding. What should the project manager do to establish a starting point before resuming project activities?

一位新的项目经理加入一个正在进行的项目。在知识转移会议上，项目经理意识到业务需求不明确，与前项目经理的理解不同。在继续项目活动之前，项目经理应该做什么来确定起点？

A、 Review the requirements management plan, stakeholder register, and functional Requirements 审查需求管理计划，相关方登记册和功能需求

B、 Read the project management plan, project charter, and business documents. 阅读项目管理计划、项目章程和商业文件

C、 Refer to the work breakdown structure (WBS), scope, schedule, and quality planning documents 查阅工作分解结构（WBS）、范围、进度计划和质量规划文件

D、 Review the requirements traceability matrix, stakeholder management plan, and acceptance Criteria 审查需求跟踪矩阵、相关方管理计划和验收标准
