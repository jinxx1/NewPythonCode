126、A project is underway to implement technology that will provide an organization with a competition advantage. The project manager learns that a newer technology was just launched that many render the planned technology roll out obsolete. What should the project manager do?

D、 completes the impact assessment and recommends the best alternative for the project 完成影响评估并为项目建议最佳可选方案
