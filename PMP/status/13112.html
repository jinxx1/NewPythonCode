44、Project team members are used to performing changes outside of the established change control process. In a team meeting, another change request was issued to the company board of directors. What should the project manager do?

项目团队成员习惯于在制定好的变更控制过程之外进行变更。在一次团队会议上，向公司董事会发出另一个变更请求。项目经理应该怎么做？

A、 Accept the change request and continue to manage the project to meet its requirements and schedule 接受该变更请求，并继续管理项目以满足其要求和进度计划

B、 Notify the requester that the change should be handled through the change control process 通知请求者应该通过变更控制过程来处理变更

C、 Conduct a team-building session and hope that the team gets back on track after all thechanges 召开一次团队建设会议，希望团队在所有变更之后重新走回正轨

D、 Ask the project sponsor for details about changes and project performance 向项目发起人询问有关变更和项目绩效的详细信息
