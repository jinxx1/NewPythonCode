49、During a monthly project status meeting, the project manager learns that the local government announced funding that could affect the Project. What should the project manager do next?

在月度项目状态会上，项目经理得知当地政府所公布的资金可能影响项目。项目经理下一步应该怎么做？

A、 Put the project on hold, as the funding may extend or enhance the project deliverables. 暂停项目，因为资金可能会延期或增强项目可交付成果

B、 Update the risk register with this information and add it to the agenda for discussion at the next risk meeting. 将这些信息更新到风险登记册中，并将其添加到到下次风险会议的讨论议程中

C、 Inform the prefect sponsor of the new opportunity and initiate a change request. 向项目发起人通知这个新机会，并提出一项变更请求

D、 Appoint a team member to request more information about the funding and what steps are needed to quality. 指定一名团队成员请求获得关于该资金的详细信息，以及需要采取哪些步骤才能获得资格
