54、A specialized resource is contracted for a project. Upon review of the project scope, the project manager and the contractor determine that the procurement group should have selected a time and material (T&M), rather than a firm fixed price (FFP) contract. What should the projectnmanager do?

项目合同规定了专业资源。在审查项目范围时，项目经理和承包商确定采购小组应选择工料合同（T&M），而不是固定总价（FFP）合同。项目经理应该怎么做？

A、 Inform the contractor that the contract cannot be changed since it is a binding and legal document. 通知承包商合同不能变更，因为合同是具有约束力的法律文件

B、 Seek approval of a change request to amend the contract 寻求批准变更请求，修改合同

C、 Perform a variance analysis as part of the Control Costs process 执行偏差分析，作为控制成本过程的组成部分

D、 Wait for the procurement group to initiate a contract change 等待采购小组提出合同变更
