43、A project manager completes plans for a project and submits all documents for approval.No time is allocated for delays in approvals. What should the project manager have done to address this potential risk?

项目经理完成项目计划并提交所有文件以供审批，没有分配延迟审批的时间。若要解决这个潜在风险，项目经理应该事先做什么？

A、 Selected a risk owner and incorporated owner-identified actions into the planning documents 选择一名风险责任人并将该责任人确定的行动纳入计划文件中

B、 Shared this potential risk information with the project stakeholders to accelerate approvals 与项目相关方分享这个潜在风险信息，以加快审批

C、 Planned specific actions, including primary and backup strategies, and chosen a risk owner 计划具体的行动，包括主要和备用策略，并选择一名风险责任人

D、 Started the project in advance to avoid impacting the schedule 提前启动项目，以避免影响进度
