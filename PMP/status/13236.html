168、A project is in its execution phase, and some deliverables are almost ready to go to the client. Team members want to quickly hand off these deliverables to save time and receive their bonus. To achieve this quality-control-related tasks will need to be eliminated. What should the project manager do?

一个项目正处于执行阶段，一些可交付成果已经准备好交给客户。团队成员希望快速交付这些可交付成果，以节省时间并获得奖金。若要实现这个目标，就必须消除质量控制相关的任务。项目经理应该怎么做？

A、 Refer the team members to the quality management plan and report ethics to the project sponsor 让团队成员查阅质量管理计划并向项目发起人报告道德问题

B、 Update the risk register with potential unmet customer requirements 将可能不满足客户要求的风险更新到风险登记册中

C、 Finalize the project, issued a change request and request a new team due to noncompliance 完成项目，签发变更请求，并由于不合规而请求一支新团队

D、 Reports the issue to the project sponsors and management to prevent the efficiency bonus from being paid 向项目发起人和管理层报告该问题，防止支付效率奖金
